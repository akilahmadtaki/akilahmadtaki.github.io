Researcher & Educator
Hello! I am a M.Sc. student in Communication & Signal Processing at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), and a Lecturer in the Department of EEE at the University of Asia Pacific. My research interests broadly include Computer Vision, Large Language Models, Applied Machine Learning / Deep Learning,and Wireless Communication. I am working under the supervision of Prof. Shaikh Anowarul Fattah at BUET, and collaborating remotely with Dr. Fnu Suya at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville.
Currently maintaining a CGPA of 4.00/4.00 in my M.Sc. program. I also completed my B.Sc. in EEE from BUET with a CGPA of 3.85/4.00. During my undergrad, I worked on brain MRI lesion segmentation using attention-gated fusion networks. I am passionate about bridging the gap between deep learning research and real-world applications in medical imaging, vision transformers, and next-generation wireless systems.

Research Experience

LOOPE: Learnable Optimal Patch Order in Positional Embeddings for Vision Transformers

Ongoing · EEE, BUET · Supervisor: Prof. Shaikh Anowarul Fattah

Proposing a novel method to determine optimal patch ordering for spatial position embeddings in ViTs, ensuring minimal loss of positional information with computational efficiency.

Demystifying Calibration in Semi-Supervised Learning

Ongoing · University of Tennessee, Knoxville · Supervisor: Dr. Fnu Suya

Investigating selection of unlabeled data points based on proximity to labeled data in feature space to improve prediction confidence in semi-supervised settings.

Performance Prediction of MIMO Antenna for 6G Communication Using ML

Aug 2024 – Jun 2025 · EEE, DIU · Supervisor: Dr.Md. Ashraful Haque

Developed compact dual-band MIMO antenna for 6G THz communication; implemented meta learner-based stacked generalization ensemble with neural network base learner.

Attention Gated 3D Fusion Network for Multi-Perspective Brain MRI Lesion Segmentation

Jun 2023 – Jul 2024 · IRAB, BUET (Undergraduate Thesis) · Supervisor: Prof. Shaikh Anowarul Fattah

Proposed a shallow multi-axis fusion network with attention mechanism (AGFN) for fusing 2D segmentation results and extracting 3D information. Introduced slice classifier to address class imbalance.

IEEE VIP CUP — Checkerboard Denoising for OCT Images

Mar 2024 · Isfahan University of Medical Sciences · Supervisor: Dr. Hafiz Imtiaz

Developed Checkerboard Denoising method for speckle noise removal in OCT images; applied Deep Image Prior for super-resolution in ophthalmology and cardiology imaging.
